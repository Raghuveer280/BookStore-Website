e-Library Help Center

1. How to Access e-Library?

To access the e-Library, visit our website and log in using your registered credentials. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for free on same page.

2. Searching for Books

Use the search bar on the homepage to find books by title, author, or topic. You can also browse different categories to discover new books.

3. Borrowing and Returning Books

To borrow a book, click on the "Add to cart" button next to the book details. You can find your borrowed books in the "MY Bookbank" section. Return books on or before the due date to avoid late fees.

4. Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using the e-Library, please contact our support team at support@mye-library.com or call us at 1160-487983-3338.
